What is The Expert Interview Show?

It is part podcast, part content service. We interview experts with legitimate knowledge to share, then help those entrepreneurial experts create content for their own channels.

Where will the interview go?

The long-form interview will appear on the show’s YouTube channel. Your edited clips are yours to post wherever you would like, and they can be branded with your logo.

What happens after I book?

You will answer a few questions about your expertise, your audience, and the topics you want to discuss. Those answers help us customize your interview before you meet.

Who is this for?

Entrepreneurs with an existing business who want to grow their digital footprint, become more prolific, and use content to support sales.
